What is OCD?

OCD is a mental state where a person has repeated, obsessive thoughts that create anxiety. To try to feel better, they may feel forced to do certain actions or rituals (called compulsions), like checking, washing, or repeating things.

But to really understand it, you need to imagine a thought that won't leave you alone. It tells you something bad will happen if you don't do what it says. Even if you know it doesn’t make sense, it still feels real. And the only way to quiet it is to obey it... Or is it?
